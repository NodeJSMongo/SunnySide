Explore Canada

Canada is 2nd largest Country in the world by area. This Country is full of natural beauties. World's longest waterfall ('Niagra') , largest fresh-water lake ('Lake Superior') , 2nd deepest harbour ('Halifax harbour') and longest road inside a metropolitan ('The yonge') and many more great Attractions made Canada a unique Country to explore.

Province-wise Canada is more attractive. Every province is differently beautiful. From the east-coast (Atlantic provinces) Halifax is the largest city, is also known for Titanic. You can get fresh air anywhere. Visit Peggy's Cove to see beauty of Atlantic ocean or Sydney (Nova Scotia) to see Whale in real.

If you want to learn french, 'Quebec or New Brunswick' might be a good option for you.
No place in the world except 'New york' has so much cultural diversity in population. Yes, 'Toronto' is the heart of Ontario is one of the best peaceful mega city in the world.

Have you heard about prairies ? Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba are called the Prairie Provinces. These provinces are not known only for wheat, petroleum or natural gas but known for many political revolutions like 'Women Political Rights' revolution.
West coast of Canada is the Queen of Pacific ocean. Vancouver, capital of British Columbia is the 3rd best place in world for living.

Three Territores- Northwest Territory, Yukon, Nunavut with Smallest Province PEI ('Prince Edward Island') and Nine other great provinces made Canada a very unique country.

Its not about just studying and earning a degree in Canada. It also about learning from nature, people and history.
